Our Programs at a Glance

We offer software engineering training with need-based, sliding-scale, subsidized tuition and stipend scholarships, followed by six months of remote job placement or job search support for women and non-binary adults seeking economic empowerment.

Get notified about the next cohort!

Techtonica's programs are virtual technical training programs that provide the opportunity for those seeking economic empowerment to learn technical skills in a safe, inclusive space. We aim to empower those most underrepresented in tech and to help tech become more diverse and inclusive. To that end, we have been very intentional about creating a safe community, building a network, and providing personalized support, which means open communication is absolutely necessary.

We select participants who are independent but good at collaborating, motivated to succeed, committed to learning to code, flexible, curious, open-minded, and excited for long-term careers in the field, have a good attitude and a growth mindset, can calmly manage extended periods of frustration and want to pay it forward.
