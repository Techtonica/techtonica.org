Our Programs at a Glance

We offer software engineering training with need-based, sliding-scale, subsidized tuition and stipend scholarships, followed by six months of remote job placement or job search support for women and non-binary adults seeking economic empowerment.

Full-Time Program

Five to six month, full-stack PERN software engineering program with need-based, sliding-scale, subsidized tuition and stipend scholarships, followed by six months of job search support or remote job placement.

Part-Time Web Dev Foundations Program

Free, virtual 10-week introductory coding fundamentals training. The program gives more people the chance to try out coding before committing fully to a career transition and and to ease the financial risk participants take on by quitting their jobs to do a full-time program in the current difficult job market for junior engineers. Pre-requisite for eligibility to apply to the part-time Software Engineering Program.

Part-Time Software Engineering Program

26-week accelerated full-stack PERN software engineering program with need-based, sliding-scale, subsidized tuition and stipend scholarships, followed by six months of job search support or remote job placement.

Graduate Consulting Program

Offered on a rolling basis, Techtonica graduates who have successfully completed our Full-Time or Part-Time Software Engineering program are able to continue prioritizing their professional development by helping partner organizations meet their software development needs. Graduates are able to gain real-world software engineering consulting experience, including scoping projects, talking to clients, meeting deliverables, holding retrospectives, reviewing PRs, and more.

Graduate Seeker Program

Offered intermittently, job seeking Techtonica graduates work together with some structure, accountability, support, reviewing, learning, practice, and stipends to make the job search process a little easier.
