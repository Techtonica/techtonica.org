Techtonica Participant Assessment of Handbook, Agreement, & Code of Conduct

Purpose for You, the Participant:

Welcome to the Techtonica program! To ensure you have the clearest possible understanding of how our program works, your responsibilities, and the support available to you, we've created this short assessment. Its main purpose is to help you confirm your grasp of the essential information found in the Participant Handbook, Participant Agreement, and Code of Conduct.


By completing this assessment, you'll:

  • Gain Clarity: Solidify your understanding of key areas like program expectations, attendance policies, financial details (tuition and stipends), job placement support, the violation process, and community conduct guidelines.
  • Prevent Future Confusion: Address any potential misunderstandings upfront, so you can focus on your learning and growth without unnecessary frustration.
  • Ensure Success: Equip yourself with the foundational knowledge needed to navigate the program effectively and make the most of this valuable opportunity.

Think of this as a helpful tool designed to set you up for success from day one, ensuring you feel confident and well-informed about your journey with Techtonica.

How This Helps Program Staff:

  • Identify Knowledge Gaps: Quickly pinpoint common areas where participants might be experiencing confusion or uncertainty.
  • Provide Targeted Support: Understand which topics require more detailed explanation or follow-up, enabling us to offer more focused guidance and resources.
  • Proactively Address Issues: Mitigate potential frustrations or misunderstandings before they escalate, leading to a smoother and more positive experience for everyone.
  • Ensure Readiness: Confirm that all participants are entering the program with a shared and accurate understanding of its critical components, fostering a more cohesive and productive cohort environment.

Ultimately, this assessment helps us ensure that we are effectively communicating vital program information and that you, our valued participant, are fully prepared and confident as you embark on your Techtonica journey.
