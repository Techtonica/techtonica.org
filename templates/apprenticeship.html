Apprenticeship

Techtonica’s tech apprenticeship and job placement program is for Bay Area adults of underrepresented genders and low incomes.

The Program

Techtonica's apprenticeship is a free, full-time, six-month opportunity that includes hands-on tech training and job placement to local women and non-binary adults with low incomes. The first month consists of full-time training. Top performers who meet all requirements will be invited to continue for another five months with stipends.

Apprentices will do full-stack web development using JavaScript. Upon successful completion of six months, top participants are placed with partner companies.

The next apprenticeship starts in January 2018.

Eligibility

You are eligible to apply for Techtonica’s apprenticeship if you:

  • are a woman or non-binary, feminine-adjacent adult
  • are digitally literate, meaning you are very familiar and good with computers and the internet, can type 30+ WPM, and you know how to use a mouse/trackpad, browsers, and email
  • meet our socioeconomic criteria (less than $50,000 in income and net worth)
  • have housing that will be stable for the duration of the program
  • have lived in the Bay Area for at least a year
  • can legally be employed by our partner companies
  • are prepared to appear in Techtonica's media and pitch yourself to Techtonica's partners
  • have not attended a full-time coding bootcamp

Application Process

1. Fill out the interest form.

Fill out the interest form completely. Submit by 9/15 for the fall 2017 application round.

2. If invited, join one of our introductions.

Learn how a web page is made and code your own. There will be several sessions to choose from. Fall 2017's sessions will be between 9/20 and 9/24 in Oakland and San Francisco.

3. Attend a coding workshop.

There will be several workshops for you to choose from. Fall 2017's workshops will be on 10/14 and 10/15 in Oakland and San Francisco.

4. Complete the take-home challenge.

We'll give you a coding challenge to work on at home.

5. Attend an in-person assessment.

Schedule a time to meet us individually to be assessed for fit.

6. Fill out the application form.

The final step is to fill out a long application.

What We Look For

This program is very competitive; we select participants who continuously prove that they are:

  • self-motivated
  • resilient
  • logical
  • humble
  • respectful
  • persistent
  • punctual
  • responsive to feedback
  • willing to ask questions
  • long-term goal setters
  • able and willing to work with anyone
  • take pride in owning their learning
  • always curious and ready to learn and improve
  • transparent, compassionate communicators
  • hard-working and ready to make sacrifices
  • dedicated to seeing things through to the end
  • willing to meet the apprentice expectations, including abiding by Techtonica's Code of Conduct
