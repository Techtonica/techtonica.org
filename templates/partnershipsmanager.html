Openings

Techtonica is a fiscally-sponsored nonprofit that helps U.S. women and non-binary adults with low incomes overcome barriers into tech careers by providing free tech training, laptops, stipends, and job placement and support through corporate sponsorships.

Make an Impact as Our Partnerships Manager!

We are seeking a committed individual to build and manage partnerships. The Partnerships Manager's work is what makes Techtonica's programs possible, and it involves every step of the sales cycle and beyond, but for a good cause, in a more personal way, and with the benefit of Techtonica's well-connected community of participants, graduates, volunteers, donors, partner organizations, and corporate sponsors.

This position is full-time and remote in the U.S., with mostly Pacific Time business hours. Unfortunately we are unable to offer visa sponsorship. With at least 8 placements to find in the next 3-4 months, we hope to find someone to join the team as soon as possible and long-term to grow and improve the organization with us.

We are especially seeking someone who is passionate about social justice and can relate to the people we serve on multiple levels: ~85% of our full-time participants identify as BIPOC, and all of them are women or non-binary adults with low incomes.

The Techtonica team you would join is made up of just a few proactive people who are passionate about the cause, and supportive of one another and community members. We care deeply about helping our participants succeed and are always making improvements to our services.

Responsibilities:

  • Work from home up to 40 hours per week
  • Efficiently and independently identify, close, manage, and build support from new and existing partners, including placements for program participants, donations, sponsorships, mentoring, speakers, and more
  • Manage and track partner communication and tasks in an organized, documented way
  • Collaborate and openly communicate with other team and community members
  • Improve Techtonica's marketing efforts and partnership processes
  • Maintain the social media calendar and content
  • Identify and apply for grants
  • Occasionally represent Techtonica at events

What else we're looking for:

  • Proven experience in building strong relationships and caring for community with diversity and inclusion in mind
  • Friendliness, confidence, and an energetic, decisive, make-it-happen personality
  • Comfort repeatedly reaching out to people you don't yet know and making asks
  • Skillful negotiating, opportunity-spotting, adaptability, efficient communication, collaboration, giving and receiving feedback, and organizing (experience in B2B sales is a plus)
  • Ability to personally relate to Techtonica's target demographic on multiple levels
  • Efficient management and prioritization of multiple tasks and contacts simultaneously
  • Dedication to social justice and Techtonica's mission
  • Ability to formulate and take responsibility for deliverables and deliver
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English
  • Attention to detail and deadlines
  • Competence with or ability to quickly learn to use software and platforms, including macOS, LinkedIn, pipeline tools and CRMs, social media, Dropbox, Slack, and Google Workspace tools
  • Ideas for how to make processes more efficient and proactively make those changes

The Partnerships Manager will receive:

  • A Techtonica MacBook to use for work
  • $28-$35 per hour (or $58,240-$72,800 per year full-time), depending on experience and location
  • Performance-based bonuses up to 30%
  • Benefits: sick leave, 14 days of PTO, 100% health insurance that starts the first day of the month after joining

If this role sounds like a fantastic fit for you, please email info AT techtonica DOT org: 1. a link to your LinkedIn profile, 2. a sentence about why Techtonica's mission excites you, 3. how many dollars of deals you've closed or funds you've raised in what amount of time, 4. a cold outreach email you would send to try to schedule a call with a potential sponsor, and 5. two past coworkers' or managers' names and email addresses for references. If we feel you could be a good fit as well, we'll respond. Applications that don't follow these instructions will not receive responses.

Selected applicants will interview with staff and board members via video call and pitch Techtonica.
