Openings

Techtonica is a fiscally-sponsored nonprofit that helps U.S. women and non-binary adults with low incomes overcome barriers into tech careers by providing free tech training, laptops, stipends, and job placement and support through corporate sponsorships.

Make an Impact as Our Partnerships Manager!

Techtonica offers a life-changing learning experience to participants. The Techtonica team is small, spirited, and supportive. We care deeply about helping our participants become excellent software engineers and are always making improvements to our services.

We are seeking a committed individual to build and manage partnerships to make our work possible.

We are especially seeking an individual who is passionate about diversity and inclusion and social justice and can relate to the people we serve on multiple levels: ~85% of our full-time participants identify as BIPOC, and all of them are women or non-binary adults with low incomes. Unfortunately we are unable to offer visa sponsorship at this time.

The ideal candidate will be extremely passionate about Techtonica's mission and the position and have the ability to quickly create and maintain positive and helpful partnerships. We hope to find someone who can join the team long-term to grow and improve the program with us.

Responsibilities:

  • Work from home up to 40 hours per week
  • Coordinate on strategy (the program’s selling points)
  • Present Techtonica values and goals
  • Quickly and independently create, drive, and manage the sponsor pipeline through the sales cycle
  • Recruit new and existing partners to grow their involvement through donations, sponsoring, hosting, mentoring, hiring, and other opportunities
  • Manage and track partner communication and tasks in an organized, documented way
  • Improve Techtonica's marketing efforts and partnership processes
  • Maintain the social media calendar and content
  • Identify and apply for grants
  • Occasionally represent Techtonica at events

What we're looking for:

  • Proven experience in building strong relationships and caring for community
  • Friendliness, confidence, and an energetic, get-it-done personality
  • Skilled at negotiating, spotting opportunities, being adaptable, communicating efficiently, and organizing
  • Ability to efficiently manage and prioritize multiple tasks and contacts simultaneously
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English
  • Competence with or ability to quickly learn to use software and platforms, including macOS, LinkedIn, pipeline tools and CRMs, social media, Dropbox, Slack, and Google Workspace tools
  • Ideas for how to make processes more efficient and proactively make those changes
  • Dedication to social justice
  • Ability to take focused responsibility for deliverables and deliver

The Partnerships Manager will receive:

  • A Techtonica MacBook to use for work
  • $28-$35 per hour (or $58,240-$72,800 per year full-time), depending on experience and location
  • Benefits: sick leave, vacation, health insurance
  • Performance-based bonuses
